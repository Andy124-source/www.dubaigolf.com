About Us

Part of Dubai Golf, Dubai Creek Golf & Yacht Club has a prestigious history as Dubai 2nd oldest course, having opened in 1993, as well as being host to the first ever golf Academy in the Middle East. Boasting an 18-hole par 71 course, DCGYC is a thrilling challenge to any golfer. Our world-class agronomy team ensure that all 18 holes are in pristine condition for all our members and guests. Being part of Dubai Creek Resort, the club has countless activities and experiences on its doorstep.
